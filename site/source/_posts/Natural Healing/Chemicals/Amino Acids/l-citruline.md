**L-citrulline**

## Healing Properties

### Nitric Oxide Production

L-arginine (Arg) and L-citrulline (Cit) helps with the production of endogenous nitric oxide (NO).[^1]

### Kidney Health

L-citruline protects against glycerol-induced acute renal failure.
