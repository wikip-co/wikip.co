## Disease / Symptom Treatment

### Cardiovascular Disease (CVD)

Higher serum biomarkers of fruit and vegetable intake are associated with reduced cardiovascular disease mortality.[^1]

### Cancer

Higher serum biomarkers of fruit and vegetable intake are associated with reduced cancer mortality.[^1]

### All-Cause Mortality

Higher serum biomarkers of fruit and vegetable intake are associated with reduced all-cause mortality.[^1]
